OPERATION COMPOST

How to Play

Towers

Different towers help you process uneaten food. Place these around the conveyor belt to reduce overall food waste

Food

Uneaten food travels along the conveyor belt into the composting bin. Try save as much food as possible by processing them to obtain points

Money

Use money to purchase more towers to create a large system of towers

Score

Score represents how much food you saved and the real life implications of reducing food waste.
